About Me

I am Mariana Bromfield, a school psychologist, looking to become a web developer. I am currently "employed" as a stay-at-home mommy (best job ever), but I've worked in the public school system for 8 years as a school psychologist. I completed my Bachelors of Science in Psychology at the University of Central Florida. I received a Masters of Science, as well as an Education Specialist degree (both in School Psychology) from Florida State University.

I was born in the Dominican Republic, but grew up in New York City and Central Florida. I currently live in Winter Garden, FL. My hobbies include going to the movies, reading novels, and traveling. I love spending time with my family.

I am currently enrolled in the UCF Coding Bootcamp. I am super excited for this new career path!
